Dr. Fei-Fei Li

Biography

Fei-Fei Li is a Chinese woman who works in the computer science field, mostly in AI. She was born on July 3rd,1976 in Beijing, China. When she was 16, she would immigrate to The United States. After high school, she should have attended Princeton University and got a bachelor's degree in physics. She would then go to Cal Tech and get a master's degree in electrical engineering. She would then bounce around different colleges and work in them until she went back to Princeton and developed something called “ImageNet.” This application would revolutionize the way that people teach their AI due to this helping AI’s gets a feel for the real world. 

This application would enhance what AIs could accomplish and make it easier to train due to them just looking at the database.   After Stanford, Dr. Li would Google as a Google Cloud Vice president and with this job she would promote AI development and try to make better AI products. During her time at google she would work on “Project Maven” which is something that the United States department of defense wanted Fei and Google to make to intercept photos that were taken through drone cameras, and they would do this via AI. Some at Google would not like this project, so the department of security would need to ensure the Google workers know that it is not for “Offensive” purposes. Even with The Department of Security telling Li this she still states that “I do not want to weaponize AI” So she would leave Google in favor of going back to teaching at Stanford in 2018.  Dr. Li would continue to work at Stanford as a professor as well as working on projects outside of the school. Some of the things that she would work on are ImageNet which would be her star project. She would also make something called “World Labs” that would create AI-generated worlds. Dr. Li has worked on a lot when it comes to the world of Artificial Intelligence and has really got AI to the level that it is now, I really think that her nickname as the “God Mother of AI” really fits her!
